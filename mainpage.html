Hello, I'm Poppy!

Poppy, a whimsical Druid fairy with a deep connection to the Circle of Stars, enchants the forest with her ethereal glow and boundless love for nature.

Learn about my story

From where it all began...

Born under a celestial event that granted her unique star-born magic, Poppy has dedicated her life to preserving the ancient secrets of the forest and protecting its mystical creatures.

Constellations

Guided by the constellations, she uncovered hidden groves and ancient relics that held the wisdom of ages past. Her journey is one of discovery and guardianship, as she seeks to balance the harmony between the magical and natural worlds.

Prophecies

Poppy's bond with the forest spirits deepened, allowing her to commune with the stars themselves, gaining insights and prophecies. Despite her gentle nature, she has faced and overcome numerous dark forces threatening her beloved forest, proving her bravery and resilience.

Homeland

Her adventures led her to form alliances with other mystical beings, each contributing to the protection of their shared homeland. Through her travels, Poppy has become a beacon of hope and wisdom, inspiring those around her to embrace the magic of the natural world.

Now, as the forest's guardian, Poppy stands ready to protect the realms she cherishes.
